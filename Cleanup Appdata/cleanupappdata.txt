Note that this module looks at all of your previously installed docker applications to determine which appdata folders are abandoned If you have deleted the previously installed list either through Community Applications or manually, then it will not display those result In other words, if there is not a record for the folder within the previously installed apps, then you will not be prompted to delete the folder=Ten en cuenta que este módulo busca en todas tus aplicaciones docker previamente instaladas para determinar qué carpetas de appdata están abandonadas. Si has eliminado la lista previamente instalada (ya sea a través de Community Applications (CA) o manualmente), entonces no mostrará resultados. En otras palabras, si no hay un registro para la carpeta dentro de las aplicaciones previamente instaladas, entonces no se te pedirá que elimines la carpeta.
In case of a severely misconfigured app previously installed, the entire contents of a share MAY be offered up to delete It is your responsibility to confirm all of the folders being offered up to delete If you inadvertently delete a share which you actually do want to keep due to your own mistakes in configuring an application, the deletion is not reversable and the author of the plugin will take no responsiblility=En el caso de que una aplicación esté mal configurada previamente, todo el contenido de una carpeta compartida puede ser ofrecido para ser eliminado. Es tu responsabilidad comprobar todas las carpetas que se muestran para ser eliminadas. Si eliminas involuntariamente una carpeta compartida que realmente quieres mantener (debido a errores en la configuración de la aplicación), la eliminación no es reversible y el autor del plugin no asumirá ninguna responsabilidad.
